Privacy Policy for Planotrak

The Planotrak application does not collect any personal data beyond the following:

  • Push Notifications – used to send reminders about project deadlines and budget limits.
  • Local Storage – we store projects, tasks, materials, time entries, and language settings locally in your browser.

We do not transmit data to third parties and we do not use tracking or analytics.
